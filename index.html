About

I am a second year PhD student in mathematics at Louisiana State University under the supervision of Prof. Arnab Ganguly. My research interests lie in statistical inference, stochastic differential equations, and probabilistic modeling. I am particularly interested in developing mathematical and computational methods for multiscale stochastic systems, Bayesian learning, Gaussian mixture models, and reinforcement learning.

My academic journey began at Ramakrishna Mission Vidyamandira, followed by a master’s degree from Indian Institute of Technology Tirupati.
