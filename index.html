About

I am a graduate student in mathematics at Louisiana State University. My research interests include stochastic differential equations, Bayesian inference, stochastic averaging, and machine learning methods for dynamical systems.

My current work focuses on statistical inference for stochastic differential equations with averaged or integral drift structures, with an emphasis on Bayesian and machine-learning-based methods for learning effective dynamics from multiscale stochastic systems.
