About

I am a graduate student in mathematics at Louisiana State University. My research interests include stochastic differential equations, Bayesian inference, stochastic averaging, and machine learning methods for dynamical systems.

Research

My current research focuses on statistical inference for stochastic differential equations with averaged or integral drift structures. I am particularly interested in Bayesian and machine-learning-based methods for learning effective dynamics from multiscale stochastic systems.

  • Stochastic differential equations
  • Stochastic averaging and multiscale dynamics
  • Bayesian inference for dynamical systems
  • Normalizing flows and machine learning methods
